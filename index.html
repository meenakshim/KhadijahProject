Why Does Afghanistan Need Our Urgent Attention?

Afghanistan is ranked 170 out of 170 countries in the world on the global Women, Peace & Security index.

100% of women-headed households have insufficient food.

Maternal mortality, infant mortality, female suicide rates, and early marriage incidence have all increased.

Steep economic decline combined with the fact that girls are banned from attending school beyond puberty.

Many families are compelled to sell their daughters at earlier and earlier ages.

More than 30% of Afghan girls are married before they turn 18!

The most recent Taliban edict forces all women to cover their faces — and to not speak or have their voices heard in public.
